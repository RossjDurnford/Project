5 Of The Best

Poseidon

An interesting fact about Poseidon is that he was one of the leading deities in Ancient Greece and was known for his violent temper. Although thought as of the God of the Sea Poseidon was actually considered the God of horses by the Greeks, he was also the God of Earthquakes and Storms. The weapon used by this fearsome God was the Trident, which is essentially a three Horned spear. The name Poseidon means Husband or Lord of the Earth.

Ares

Ares is the Greek God of War, Bloodshed and Violence. There is a story that intertwines Aphrodite and Ares as lovers. However Aphrodite fell in love with a mortal named Adonis. Ares then through his jealousy killed Adonis. Ares was also the son of Zeus Adonis and Hera. Additionally he was one of the favoured Gods by the Spartans due to his bloodthirst and in art he is often seen as a warrior ready for battle.

Zeus

Zeus is the God of Sky and Thunder, who rules as the king of the Gods on Mount Olympus. Zeus lead the Olympians to victory against the Titans. One of the Titans was also Zeus's father Cronus. Zeus also married his sister Hera and had children by the names of, Ares, Hebe and Hephaestus. Another interesting fact about Zeus is that the Olympic Games were held in his honor in Athens, where each of the Greek Islands would assign an athlete to compete.

Hermes

Hermes was the Greek messenger of the Gods, you may recognise the name from the parcel service. he is also considered the protector of human heralds, travellers, merchants, thieves and orators. Hermes had access to the underowlrd which alot of people including Gods did not, additionally he also had 2 children with Aphrodite and was considered to be a great inventor.

Hephaestus

Hephaestus is the Greek god of fire, the forge and blacksmiths. His Roman counterpart was known as Vulcan. Hephaestus was raised by mortals after Hera tossed him from Mount Olympus. he was then raised on the Island of Lemnos. During his time there he became a master craftsman and crafted a Golden Throne for the Gods. Once Hera his Mother sat in the Throne it ensnared her and she was unable to break free. After this he became the blacksmith for the gods. hephaestus also created Pandora, however after a time Zeus used Pandora to punish Prometheus for stealing fire from the gods and giving it to Mankind.
